About Us

Ingenious Instruments is a scientific device development venture that intends to design and market scientific devices for healthcare diagnosis, scientific education and academic research. Currently, a low cost, automated, high precision microscope has been designed and prototyped and is being tested in a research facility at Cambridge University.
